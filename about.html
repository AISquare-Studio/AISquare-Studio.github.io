Our Story

"Founded in 2023, AISquare set out to make learning engaging and impactful. Starting with Math Premier League (MPL), an ELO-based skill assessment and challenge game, we unlocked the potential of adaptive learning. Inspired by this, we developed AI-driven Quizzes that adjust to each user’s skill level. Today, AISquare empowers individuals and communities to learn, teach, and grow through AI-powered experiences."

Our Vision

We envision a world where AI empowers people to unlock their full potential, both as individuals and as a community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to supercharge skill development through AI-driven innovation, transforming the way people learn, collaborate, and grow.

Our Values

Responsible Ownership

We believe in proactive transparency and accountability. We treat every project as if it’s our own and keep the team and stakeholders informed and engaged, empowering everyone to make informed decisions and drive meaningful results.

Inclusive Innovation

We embrace diversity and foster a culture where all voices contribute to innovation. Supporting growth, learning from varied perspectives, and building trust are at our core. Our products and services empower individuals and institutions of all types and sizes in their journey toward growth.

Driven by Simplicity

Simplicity is at the heart of our approach. We cut through complexity to deliver clear, focused solutions that make an impact. Every process and product we create is designed to be intuitive and effective for our customers and teams alike.

Purposeful & Results-Oriented

Speed and focus drive us forward. We act with urgency and clear goals, ensuring that each initiative, big or small, creates measurable impact. By staying results-driven and purposefully aligned with our mission, we build a legacy of meaningful, positive change.
